About Madhusudanji

Madhusudan Nade is an Educated Yoga Professional and a Certified Yoga Alliance Teacher. With more than 20 years of experience in practicing Yoga, he has mastered the art of Yoga and finds happiness in making others positive and solving their problems through Meditation and Yoga.
He has a holistic approach to Yoga and believes that the presence of mind and the support of body remain equally important throughout the process of healing. Madhusudanji's ardour for Yoga makes him stand out. He believes in curing all sorts of problems of people through their minds and ensures that everyone leaves her class refreshed and empowered.
